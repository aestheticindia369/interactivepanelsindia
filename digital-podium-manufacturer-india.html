What is a Digital Podium?

A digital podium, also known as a smart lectern, is an advanced presentation system that integrates a touch screen display, computer system, microphone, audio controls and connectivity interfaces into a single unit. It is designed to enhance teaching, training and presentation experiences in educational and professional environments.

Digital podiums eliminate the need for multiple devices by providing a centralized control system for presentations, multimedia content and interactive communication.

Applications of Digital Podiums

  • Government training institutes and administrative centres
  • Corporate conference rooms and boardrooms
  • Seminar halls and auditoriums
  • Defence training facilities and briefing rooms
