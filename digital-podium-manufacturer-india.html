What is a Digital Podium?

A digital podium is an advanced all-in-one presentation system that integrates a touch display, computing unit, microphone system and AV connectivity into a single cabinet. It replaces the traditional lectern with a smart, interactive command centre that enhances teaching and presentation delivery.

It centralises control for projectors, displays, lighting, audio and video — enabling the presenter to focus entirely on their audience without juggling multiple devices or cables.
