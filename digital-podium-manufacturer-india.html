About Our Podiums

What is a Digital Podium?

A digital podium is the ultimate command centre for the modern presenter. Unlike a traditional wooden lectern, an Aesthetic India digital podium integrates a high-resolution touch display, a powerful built-in computer, microphone system, HDMI/USB AV switching and a secure lockable cabinet — all within one sleek, professional unit.

Whether you're a professor delivering a lecture to 300 students, a corporate executive presenting to a boardroom, or a government official chairing a conference — our digital podiums give you complete control over every element of your presentation without juggling cables, remotes or multiple devices.

Aesthetic India is a leading digital podium manufacturer and supplier in India, serving 500+ institutions across Delhi, Uttar Pradesh, Uttarakhand, Haryana, Rajasthan, Maharashtra and all major states. We offer four distinct podium types — each engineered for a specific use case and budget.

Complete Audio Solution

Speaker & Amplifier
Systems

A digital podium is only as good as the audio system it connects to. Aesthetic India designs and installs complete PA (Public Address) audio systems for classrooms, lecture halls, conference rooms and auditoriums — ensuring every word from the podium microphone reaches every seat in the room with clarity and power.

Our audio systems are engineered as zones — with independent volume control for different areas of the room. A typical large lecture hall setup includes: a zone amplifier (240W–480W), wall-mounted or ceiling speakers for each zone, a wireless UHF microphone receiver at the podium, and an audio DSP processor for echo cancellation, feedback suppression and EQ tuning. The entire system integrates with the podium's audio output via balanced XLR connections.

  • Zone amplifiers: 240W, 360W & 480W options
  • Wall-mount & ceiling speakers (8Ω, 16Ω, 100V line)
  • Wireless UHF handheld & clip-on microphone systems
  • DSP processor: echo cancel, feedback suppression, EQ
  • Touch panel volume control per zone
  • Full rack installation with cable management

What is the difference between an audio podium and a digital podium? +

An audio podium (also called an audio lectern) is a traditional presentation stand fitted with built-in microphones, an amplifier and speakers — optimised for voice amplification. A digital podium, on the other hand, adds a built-in touch screen display, a computer (Windows PC), HDMI/USB AV switching capabilities and full AV matrix control — making it an all-in-one interactive presenter console. Many institutions start with an audio podium and upgrade to a digital podium as their AV needs grow.
