About Our Interactive Flat Panels

What is an Interactive Flat Panel & Why Does Your Institution Need One?

An Interactive Flat Panel (IFP) is a next-generation large-format touchscreen display that replaces traditional blackboards, projectors and whiteboards in classrooms, boardrooms and training centres. Unlike old projector-based setups — which suffer from shadows, bulb replacements, poor resolution in bright rooms and constant maintenance — an Interactive Flat Panel delivers a crystal-clear 4K image you can write on, annotate, draw on and control directly with your fingers or stylus.

Aesthetic India is one of India's leading Interactive Flat Panel manufacturers and suppliers, trusted by government schools, private institutions, defence training centres and corporate boardrooms across Delhi, UP, Uttarakhand and every state in India. Our IFPs combine ultra-high-definition display technology, precision infrared multi-touch and powerful on-board computing in one elegant, low-maintenance unit built to last 10+ years.

Whether you are looking to create a smart classroom, upgrade a corporate meeting room, build a command-and-control centre or set up a government training facility, Aesthetic India has the right Interactive Flat Panel — at the most competitive price in India — with full on-site support, installation and AMC.

Step-by-Step Guide

How Does an Interactive Flat Panel Work?
A Complete Explainer

Understanding the technology behind your IFP helps you get the most out of it. Here is a complete, in-depth guide to how our panels work — from display to touch to content delivery.

1

4K UHD LCD Display Panel

The core is a commercial-grade 4K LCD panel rated for 50,000+ hours of continuous operation. Unlike TV panels, it has higher brightness (up to 450 cd/m²), wider viewing angles (178°) and anti-glare treatment — so it looks perfect even in brightly lit classrooms with windows.

2

Infrared Multi-Touch Frame

An invisible grid of infrared (IR) LEDs surrounds the screen edges. When a finger, stylus or palm interrupts the IR beams, the panel instantly calculates the exact touch coordinates with ≤1mm accuracy and <4ms response. Up to 40 simultaneous touches are supported on Windows, 20 on Android — enabling multi-student collaborative exercises.

3

Zero-Bonding Glass Layer

A premium zero-bonding process permanently bonds the 4mm anti-glare tempered glass directly to the LCD panel with optical glue — eliminating the air gap. This removes parallax (the offset between where you touch and where the cursor appears), reduces glare and gives a natural pen-on-paper writing feel.

4

On-Board Android 14 Computer

An Amlogic T982 Quad/Octa Core processor with 4GB–8GB RAM and 32GB–128GB storage runs Android 14 natively inside the panel. This means you can run teaching apps, YouTube, Google Classroom, ERP software and K-12 content directly — no separate laptop or PC required. OTA (over-the-air) software updates keep it current.

5

Windows via OPS Module

For institutions needing full Windows 11 — press one button to switch from Android to Windows. The OPS (Open Pluggable Specification) slot accepts any standard OPS PC module, giving you a full Intel Core Windows computer inside the panel. Switch back to Android with one tap. No reboots. No extra monitors.

6

Wireless Casting & Collaboration

Using Miracast, Airplay or proprietary casting apps, up to 4 devices can cast their screens to the panel simultaneously — split screen. A student can share their Android phone, another casts from a MacBook, and the teacher controls from the panel itself. WiFi 6 dual-band (2.4GHz + 5GHz) ensures no lag.

7

K-12 Digital Content Platform

Pre-installed on every Aesthetic India IFP is our comprehensive K-12 digital content suite — 2 million+ resources covering CBSE, ICSE and all major state boards from Nursery to Class 12. Teachers can search by chapter, lesson or topic and instantly display animated videos, 3D models, quizzes and interactive exercises mapped to the curriculum.

8

Cloud, LAN & Remote Management

IFPs connect to your school's or office's LAN via Ethernet or WiFi. Admins can remotely manage all panels across the campus — push software updates, control content access and monitor usage — from a central dashboard. OTA updates ensure the latest features arrive automatically.

Frequently Asked Questions — Interactive Flat Panels in India

What is the difference between an Interactive Flat Panel and a Smart TV?+
An Interactive Flat Panel (IFP) is a commercial-grade display built for education and enterprise — not home entertainment. Key differences: IFPs have higher brightness (350–450 cd/m² vs 200–300 in TVs), anti-glare glass (essential in lit classrooms), 40-point multi-touch, built-in computing (Android + Windows OPS), longer screen life (50,000 hours vs 30,000), HDMI loop-out, RS232 control and zero-bonding touch technology. Smart TVs have none of these. IFPs also come with 3-year on-site warranties, whereas TVs typically offer only 1 year carry-in warranty.

As of 2026, 65-inch IFPs typically start from ₹75,000–₹1,00,000, 75-inch from ₹95,000–₹1,30,000, 86-inch from ₹1,30,000–₹1,80,000, and 98-inch from ₹2,00,000+. Which size IFP is best for a standard classroom?+
For a standard classroom of 30–40 students, a 75-inch Interactive Flat Panel is ideal — it provides excellent visibility from all corners without being overwhelming. For larger classrooms with 40–60 students, go for an 86-inch. Smaller group rooms or labs do well with a 65-inch.
