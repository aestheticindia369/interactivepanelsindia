Component Deep Dive — 04

PTZ Camera — Online & Hybrid Classes

Post-COVID, every Indian institution needs to support hybrid learning — some students in the room, others joining online. A PTZ (Pan-Tilt-Zoom) camera with AI auto-tracking captures the teacher automatically as they move, delivering professional-quality video to Zoom, Teams or Google Meet without any camera operator.

  • ▸20x optical zoom — captures teacher face from 10m away
  • ▸AI auto-tracking — follows teacher without operator
  • ▸1080p Full HD — crystal clear for remote students
  • ▸USB + HDMI — plug and play with IFP
  • ▸Works with Zoom, Teams, Google Meet natively
ℹ PTZ Camera Placement in Indian Classrooms

Rear wall centre is the best position for most classrooms — it gives a wide view of the teacher and board from 8–10m away. The 20x optical zoom handles this distance perfectly. Mount at or slightly above IFP height for natural eye contact in video calls.

In India, "smart board" and "interactive flat panel (IFP)" are used interchangeably. Technically, "SMART Board" is a brand name. An IFP is the modern, superior technology — it is a large 4K LED display with integrated touch, built-in Android/Windows PC and wireless connectivity. It replaces the older projector-based interactive whiteboard systems that required calibration, lamp replacement and are dim in daylight. Aesthetic India's IFPs are superior to legacy smart board technology.
