Product Overview

What is an IR Touch Frame?

An IR Touch Frame (Infrared Touch Frame) is a precision-engineered slim overlay frame that mounts directly over any existing flat panel display — LCD, LED, or commercial panel — and instantly adds 10-point multi-touch capability to it.

Inside the frame, rows of infrared LED emitters and photodetectors create an invisible grid of light beams across the entire display surface. The moment a finger, stylus or any opaque object touches or approaches the screen, it interrupts one or more beams. The frame's onboard processor calculates the exact XY coordinates and transmits them to the connected device via USB in real time.

The result: your existing non-touch television, LED panel or commercial display becomes a fully interactive 10-point touch screen without any hardware modification, screen replacement or complex installation.

Working Principle

How the IR Touch Frame Works

Four simple stages from physical touch to on-screen response — all happening in milliseconds.

1

Infrared Grid Created

IR LED emitters on two edges of the frame send invisible infrared beams horizontally and vertically across the entire display surface, forming a precise XY grid.

2

Touch Detected

When a finger, stylus or pen touches or closely approaches the surface, it interrupts one or more infrared beams at a specific point on the grid.

3

Coordinates Calculated

The frame's onboard microprocessor identifies exactly which beams were interrupted and calculates the precise XY touch coordinates at up to 10 simultaneous points.

4

Signal Sent via USB

Touch data is transmitted to the connected Windows, Android or Linux device via USB HID protocol in under 8 milliseconds — fast enough for smooth writing and gestures.

What Makes It Work

The infrared sensor grid built into the frame edges constantly scans for interruptions. Because it responds to any opaque object — finger, stylus or pen — it delivers accurate, smooth touch with no drivers to install. Setup is a simple 4-step process: place the frame over the display, align it, connect the USB cable, and the screen is touch-enabled immediately.

Built to Fit Any Display

The frame uses a sleek, slim profile that blends seamlessly with any LCD, LED or Smart TV without altering its appearance. It requires no external power adapter, drawing power entirely through the USB connection, and is built with durable materials designed for long-term daily use in classrooms, offices and retail environments.

Easy Setup

4-Step Installation — Under 15 Minutes

No technician needed. No tools. No screen modification. Any teacher or office administrator can set it up independently.

Step 1

Place Frame Over Display

Align the IR Touch Frame over the existing LCD or LED display. The frame is designed to fit snugly over the display bezel with no drilling or adhesive required. Adjustable clips secure it firmly.

Step 2

Align and Secure

Adjust the frame to ensure the infrared grid is precisely aligned with the active display area. Secure the mounting clips to prevent any movement during use.

Step 3

Connect USB Cable

Plug the single USB cable from the IR Touch Frame into any available USB port on your computer, laptop, or Android device. The HID driver loads automatically — no software installation needed.

Step 4

Ready to Use

Your display is now fully touch-enabled. Open your preferred teaching app, whiteboard software or presentation tool and start using multi-touch gestures immediately.
